Bonfire History:

Did you know that bonfires were originally created by the Mayan Empire?

They were thought to scare away

  • Spiders
  • Giraffes
  • The Spanish
which the Mayans thought to be creatures of darkness.

Personally, I think they were right about at least two of those things.
